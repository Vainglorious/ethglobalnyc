PREDICTIONS LEDGER — plain-text companion to /predictions.json
================================================================

What this is
------------
predictions.json (repo root) is the machine-readable ledger of every Colony
prediction-market trade. Values are hard on-chain data (Polygon), verified
against the transaction receipts. polygun/record_trade.py is the script that
decodes a tx and appends an entry here.

This .txt is the human-readable mirror — skim it instead of parsing JSON.

Venue: PolyGun (custodial relayer that fronts Polymarket; sidesteps the US
geoblock). Settlement token is pUSD (Polymarket USD, 6 decimals,
0xc011a7e12a19f7b1f670d46f03b03f3342e82dfb).
