    For a still-competitive game there's no lock to scrape — betting the leader is

  KEY LESSON (the real takeaway of this whole experiment):
  The "buy the 99c winner before it settles to 100c" scrape is STRUCTURALLY
  IMPOSSIBLE at the close — the winning side has no sellers (0 asks), only bids.
  Only those who ENTERED EARLIER (when the book was two-sided) capture 99->100.
  Plan the colony's edge around its PRE/IN-GAME read while liquidity is two-sided,
  NOT around scalping the resolved winner.
