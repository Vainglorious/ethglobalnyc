LESSONS FROM QATAR-SWITZERLAND (apply here)
-------------------------------------------
1. SPEED is the bottleneck, not the trade path. The draw arb (1-1) was a ~+$55
   counterfactual we missed because the market repriced before we acted. For the
   late/arb bet we need a live-score read FASTER than the market reprice.
   (See simulatedtransactions.json.)
2. PRICE OFF THE LIVE SOURCE (PolyGun/Polymarket), not ClickHouse — CH's
   polymarket_markets_all batch lags the live game by minutes.
3. CHECK THE OUTCOME MARKET DIRECTLY before betting: a market quoting ~100c/0.1c
   (or all outcomes near 0) = resolved/closing book -> do NOT market-buy into it.
4. WINNER MARKET ONLY is executable here; O/U is discovery-only.
5. Our reads have been good (Switzerland win pre-game = in profit; under-2.5 +
   draw both correct) — keep trusting the colony, fix speed + venue.
