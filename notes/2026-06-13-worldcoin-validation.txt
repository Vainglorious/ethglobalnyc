  Every address Adil approves resolves to the SAME anonymous humanId (one human).

DESIGN INTENT (why verify these ants)
  These verified ants get PRIVILEGED status going forward: larger birth bankroll +
  premium ClickHouse data tier, and they may interact with other ants differently
  because of it (the verified-vs-anon experiment, plan §8). The humanId/verified
  badge gets stamped into colony state / ENS lineage records.

HOW WE'RE RUNNING IT (decided)
  Adil runs `register <addr>` LOCALLY and scans the QR / approves in World App for
  each one. Running locally avoids the session-mismatch timeout we hit when Claude
  ran register in the background (each register opens its OWN session; the QR you
  scan must be the session that command opened). Claude provides the commands +
  verifies status after.

    Dev 2   -> ant_0005..ant_0009  (-> Dev 2's DIFFERENT humanId)
  This yields TWO distinct verified lineage roots (real N=2), not one — closer to
  the verified-vs-anon experiment's intent.
