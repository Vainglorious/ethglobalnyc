UMA RESOLUTION CYCLE — WHAT TO WATCH (the learning goal)
-------------------------------------------------------
Polymarket settles via UMA Optimistic Oracle V2. Expected event sequence per market
(table: umalabs.uma_oo_v2_events_decoded, event_name):
  RequestPrice  -> Polymarket asks UMA to resolve (fires after the game/end_time)
  ProposePrice  -> a proposer submits the outcome
  DisputePrice  -> (optional) someone challenges within the liveness window
  Settle        -> outcome finalized; market price -> 1.00 / 0.00, closed=true
THE ARB EDGE (Bet 3): act in the gap between ProposePrice and Settle, once the
proposed outcome is visible on-chain but before the market/UI fully reflects it.
