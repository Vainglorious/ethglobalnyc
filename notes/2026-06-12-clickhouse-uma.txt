WHAT THIS LAYER IS
  The first layer of ClickHouse access for the Colony agents. We call it the
  "uma-clickhouse-oracle" data layer. The umalabs database is experimental and
  good for the hackathon, so agents get FREE RANGE over it: read it, and even
  modify it (insert / update / delete / create / drop tables and views). The
  Polymarket reference tables are read-only by contrast.
