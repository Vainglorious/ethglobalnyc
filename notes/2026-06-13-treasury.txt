PROVEN: UNLINK PRIVATE TRANSFER FUNDED BY THE TREASURY (2026-06-13)
  The Unlink faucet does NOT dispense Arc USDC ("token not supported by faucet"), so we deposit
  real USDC from the treasury instead. Full lifecycle worked end-to-end on arc-testnet:
    treasury 0x1be3...2D8F --depositWithApproval 2 USDC--> Wallet A (private)
    Wallet A --transfer 1 USDC--> Wallet B (private)
    Result: A 2->1 USDC, B 0->1 USDC, both status "processed". 1 USDC moved privately ✅
  Script: unlink/deposit-and-transfer.mjs (viem walletClient over Arc RPC + evm.fromViem,
  then depositWithApproval + transfer). Details in notes/2026-06-13-unlink-exploration.txt.
