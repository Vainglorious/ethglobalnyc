# The visualization you'll see on screen, in plain English

*For the product side. This is the thing the audience actually watches.*

## What it is
A living **3D world** — "COLONY: Living Intelligence" — where the whole forecasting swarm becomes
something you can see and fly around. Ants move over hills and water; little glowing pulses fly
between them when they "debate"; streams of light arc from ants to their nests when they place
stakes. It's the colony's thinking, made visible.

## What the visuals mean
- **The ants** — each one is a forecasting agent. **Verified (human-backed) ants glow gold.**
- **Pulses between ants** — agents sharing opinions during a debate.
- **Light streams to the nest** — money being staked; winners' streams rush in, losers' fade out.
- **Worn paths on the ground** — the trails the colony has been using most (like real ant trails).
- **Click an ant or a nest** — a card pops up with its live stats (bankroll, accuracy, generation,
  etc.).

## Where the data comes from
Two modes:
- **Replay** — it plays back a real recorded run from the colony's brain (the Python engine).
- **Live-ish local** — an always-on built-in simulation so the world still feels alive even with
  nothing else running.

The two sides share one strict agreement (a data "contract") so the picture always matches what
the brain actually produced. (A true real-time live feed is the next step — the wiring exists but
isn't switched on yet.)

## What works today vs. what's coming
- **Works now:** the full 3D world, ants moving, debate pulses, stake flows, trails, two camera
  modes (walk around vs. overhead), clickable info cards, and a slider to scale up to ~2,000 ants
  at a smooth frame rate.
- **Coming:** showing ants being **born and dying** on screen, and — the big one — the **survival
  chart** the plan calls the demo's strongest moment: *do the privileged human-verified family
  lines win over time, or do the lean anonymous ones out-compete them?*

## One-line summary
**It turns the whole colony into a living 3D world you can fly through — ants, debates, and money
flows you can watch — driven by the real forecasting engine, with the headline survival chart
coming next.**
